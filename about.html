About GS Flooring

Your trusted partner in quality flooring since 2020

Who We Are

GS Flooring is the creation of Gary Stephenson, a flooring enthusiast from Kidderminster and now based in Upton-Upon-Severn. With a passion for quality craftsmanship and exceptional service, Gary founded GS Flooring to bring premium flooring solutions to the community.

Our Heritage

Kidderminster has a storied legacy as a world-renowned carpet manufacturing hub. This rich heritage of textile excellence and craftsmanship has deeply influenced Gary's approach to flooring. Just as Kidderminster became famous for producing the finest carpets, GS Flooring is committed to delivering that same level of quality, attention to detail, and pride in craftsmanship to every project we undertake.

About Gary Stephenson

Gary is not just a flooring expert, but also a soul music lover and classic scooter enthusiast. He shares an appreciation for the iconic Lambretta scooters, which represent the same values that drive his business: style, quality, and reliability. When he's not helping customers find the perfect flooring solution.
